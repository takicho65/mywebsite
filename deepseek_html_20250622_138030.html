About Me

I am an Ethiopian agronomist specializing in crop physiology, seed systems, and nutrient management. Currently pursuing my PhD in Crop Science at Hunan Agricultural University, China, while serving as an Associate Researcher at the Ethiopian Institute of Agricultural Research (EIAR).

Education

  • PhD in Crop Science (2024-2028) - Hunan Agricultural University, China
  • MSc in Agronomy (2015-2017) - Ambo University, Ethiopia
  • BSc in Plant Science (2010-2014) - Ambo University, Ethiopia

Research Focus

Seed Systems Technology

Developing optimized protocols for chickpea and tef production in Ethiopian Vertisols.

Climate-Resilient Crops

Participatory selection of drought-tolerant varieties for smallholder farmers.

Precision Nutrient Management

Phosphorus and nitrogen optimization in cereal-legume systems.
